Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What is the duration of your regular classes?

Our regular classes run for 2 hours per session, typically once or twice a week depending on the subject and level. The complete course duration covers the entire academic year with special revision sessions before exams.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including notes, past papers with solutions, model papers, and revision guides. All materials are carefully prepared by our expert tutors to match the syllabus and exam requirements.

Can I join online classes if I'm already attending physical classes?

Absolutely! Many of our students combine both physical and online classes for maximum benefit. The online platform gives you access to recorded lectures and additional resources that complement the physical classes.

How do I enroll in your programs?

You can enroll by visiting any of our centers, contacting us via phone, or through our WhatsApp numbers provided on this website. We'll guide you through the registration process and help you choose the right program for your needs.
